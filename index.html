Someone Like You Survey App

Someone Like You is a survey app with a twist. Users create an account and sign in before taken to our first questionnaire where we ask some basic information from the user. From there the user is taken to their dashboard where they can pick any question they like from our list. We have questions on a varitety of topics. After a user answers a question they are taken to a results page showing the user how their answer compares with other user's answers. Users are also able to go back and look at questions that they previously answered and are able to see the data including users who answered after the original user.

Tech Stack: React, CSS, Node, Express, PostgreSQL
